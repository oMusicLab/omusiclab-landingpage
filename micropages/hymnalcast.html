HymnalCast

Lightweight offline lyrics and slides for worship and events — library, presenter, and remote control in one flow.

Not published yet

HymnalCast is an early LitePresenter experiment. We are shaping the offline library and presenter experience before a public release.

What we are building

Hymn library

Organize lyrics and arrangements in one place — browse, search, and keep sets ready for rehearsal or Sunday morning.

Presenter view

Clean slide output for projectors and displays, tuned for readable lyrics during worship and live events.

Remote control

Advance slides from a phone or tablet on the same network so the person at the console is not tied to the keyboard.

Offline-first

Built for venues with spotty Wi‑Fi — load your library once and present without depending on a live connection.

Intended flow

  1. 1
    Build your library Import or arrange hymns and lyric slides for upcoming services and events.
  2. 2
    Queue a set Pick songs for the order of worship and preview how they will appear on screen.
  3. 3
    Present and control Send lyrics to the projector while advancing from a separate remote device.
